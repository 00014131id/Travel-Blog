  • Bali

    Also known as the Land of the Gods, Bali appeals through its sheer natural beauty of looming volcanoes and lush terraced rice fields that exude peace and serenity. There is no other place like Bali in this world. Bali is often rated as one of the best travel destinations in the world.

  • Batur

    The captivating Mount Batur surrounds the 13-sq-km Batur caldera lake. Those with a penchant for adventure can take a winding road down to the lakeshore. This leads you to Toya Bungkah, Ulun Danu Batur temple, and a collection of hot springs.

  • Japan

    Japan! The land of sushi, the Tori and the sumo wrestling! But for us, Japan also means incredibly sweet people, lots of hiking and the impressive and beautiful city of Hiroshima. Japan is an archipelago, or string of islands, on the eastern edge of Asia.

  • Himeji

    The first fortifications built on the site were completed in the 1400s, and were gradually enlarged over the centuries by the various clans who ruled over the region. The castle complex is more than 400 years old.

  • Canada

    Canada is a vast and rugged land. From north to south, across six time zones, it spans more than half the Northern Hemisphere, the second largest country in the world, but with only one-half of one percent of the world's population. It borders US in the west and US in the south.

  • Niagara

    Niagara Falls is Canada's most famous natural attraction, bringing in millions of visitors each year. Located just over an hour's drive from Toronto, along the American border, these massive falls drop approximately 57 meters.
